Digital Illustration

Since 2012, I've been creating digital artwork, and starting in 2016 I began working with independant clients to create personalized artwork and portraits. As an artist, I am inspiried by the intersection of science and art: human biology, ornothology, oceanography, etc.

I primarily use a Wacom Intuos tablet, and Adobe Photohop 2020.

Revolution Brewing

For 2 years, I was lucky to be part of Revolution Brewing, Illinois' largest independantly-owned craft brewery. While there, I was able to learn every part of the craft beer lifecycle: from brewing alongside our head-brewer; to marketing, packaging, and taste-testing beer; to serving it for real people everyday on the floor.

It's where I developed my love of understanding every nut and bolt about a subject-- and a love of beer! Cheers!
